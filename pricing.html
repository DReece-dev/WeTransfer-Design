FAQ

What Happens after I sign up and pay?

You’ll be able to get started with WeTransfer Pro or WeTransfer Premium as soon as you’ve completed your payment. We’ll also send you a confirmation email with your paid invoice attached.

Once you’re in, you’ll be able to send bigger transfers, start using portals, and create your very own WeTransfer page right away. If you have any previous transfers that were sent while you were on the free plan, you’ll also be able to change the expiration dates to whenever you like.

Do my recipients need to sign up?

When you use WeTransfer, anyone can download your files—there’s no need for them to log in or sign up. They’ll have to accept our Terms of Service and we may ask them to accept cookies, but that’s it!

If you want to share work or collaborate in portals (WeTransfer’s latest feature), your clients and collaborators won’t need to sign up to view and select files, add comments, give approval, or download files. You’ll only need to add team members to your Pro or Premium plan if you want people to be able to add reviews and create portals with you.

Can I receive bigger transfers from anyone?

With a Pro or Premium plan, you can create your own WeTransfer page where anyone can send you files with the transfer size limit of your plan. That means you can get the large files you need from clients or collaborators, and they won’t need to have an account—just the URL to your page. (Though we may ask them to verify their email address to protect you from “foreign princes” who “want to send you money”).

How do I add (and remove) team members?

First, you’ll need to sign up for WeTransfer Pro or WeTransfer Premium—you can’t add team members while you’re on the free plan. And whichever plan you choose, your team members will enjoy all the same benefits (so you can’t get Premium for yourself and have some team members on Pro, for example). Team members will be able to send and receive bigger transfers from your WeTransfer page, and manage transfers from one shared overview.

Once you’ve signed up for the plan that’s best for your team, you’ll be able to invite as many team members as you like from your team management settings. You can add and remove members from your team at any time while your subscription is active.

What payment methods can I use?

We accept PayPal as well as credit cards by major networks such as Visa, Mastercard, and American Express. We also support other online payment systems in some countries.

We don’t accept direct bank transfers because you have to sign up for a recurring yearly or monthly subscription. (We’ll email you before this happens for the first time, to give you a heads up.)

What happens if I go over my storage limit?

If you don’t have the unlimited storage with WeTransfer Premium, you’ll have to either clear up some storage space by deleting some of your sent transfers or upgrade your plan. Until you do, you won’t be able to send new transfers.

If you’re using WeTransfer for free, all your transfers of up to 2 GB are automatically deleted after 7 days. So don’t worry, it’s not possible for you to go over your storage limit.

How do I cancel?

Your subscription will automatically renew (we’ll email you before this happens for the first time), but you can cancel at any time.

To switch to using WeTransfer for free, all you need to do is go to your Account settings and cancel your subscription. You (and any team members you might have) will be able to continue using your Pro or Premium plan until the end of your current subscription period.

Can I trust WeTransfer? How safe is it?

Millions of people around the world trust WeTransfer and our set of tools to share their ideas—with more than 2 billion files sent through our platform every month.

To keep your ideas safe from attack or exploitation, your files are encrypted when they are transferred (TLS) and when they are stored (AES-256). Once your files are stored, they can only be accessed using the unique links sent to the sender and recipient.
